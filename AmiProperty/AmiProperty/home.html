Tenant Registration Night @ Mercure Hotel

amiProperty is hosting a tenant registration night in the Mercure Hotel on Ingram Street Glasgow, 7pm till 9pm on Tuesday 4th March.

This night is aimed at anyone who would be looking to rent a flat in Glasgow, Renfrewshire or surrounding areas over the next 3 months. In order to register you will need one form of photographic ID, one proof of current address and the details of a referee who may be contacted for either an employment or personal reference. amiProperty will then be targeting landlords in the areas YOU want to live - taking the stress out of property hunting for you and ensuring that you have first refusal on any properties on our books and access to properties that haven't even been listed yet! The entire process is completely free and your details will only be used by ami to find you your perfect next home. No other letting agency will give you such a personal service in trying to find your new home so why not come along, meet the team and sign up to this fantastic opportunity to let the letting agent work for you for a change!

For further information please contact us.
